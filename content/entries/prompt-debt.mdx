  The future cost of every expedient line added to a prompt because shipping today felt nobler than understanding tomorrow.
plainDefinition: |
  Prompt debt is the accumulated maintenance burden, fragility, and hidden coupling created when prompts, examples, and guardrails are patched repeatedly without clear structure, tests, ownership, or retirement.
whyExists: |
  Prompting is fast, legible, and dangerously hospitable to tactical fixes. Teams keep adding caveats, examples, and exceptions because it works now, while the eventual complexity invoice waits with unusual patience.
misuse: |
  It gets romanticised as artisanal prompt craft or dismissed as not real engineering because it lives in text. In truth, prompt debt is ordinary technical debt wearing conversational clothing.
practicalMeaning: |
  In practice it means contradictory instructions, duplicated examples, stale references, unexplained magic phrases, harder debugging, slower onboarding, and a model that obeys yesterday's emergency patch long after everyone forgets why it was invited.

Prompt debt matters because prompts are now product logic by other means. Treating them as disposable notes is a good way to acquire a permanent archaeology project.
