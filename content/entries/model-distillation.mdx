plainDefinition: |
  Model distillation trains a student model to match a teacher model's outputs or behaviours, transferring capability into a smaller or cheaper model.
whyExists: |
  Large models are costly to serve. Distillation is a primary way to compress capability for deployment.
misuse: |
  Assumed complete transfer. Students miss long tails, tool skills, and safety behaviours unless those are explicitly distilled and tested.
practicalMeaning: |
  Distil on task distributions you care about, evaluate safety and capability separately, and monitor production divergence from the teacher.
