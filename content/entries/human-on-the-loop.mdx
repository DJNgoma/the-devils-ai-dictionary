  Human-on-the-loop describes oversight where a person monitors an automated or agentic system and can intervene, without approving every individual step as in human-in-the-loop designs.
whyExists: |
  Full step approval does not scale; full autonomy scares risk owners. On-the-loop is the middle governance posture.
misuse: |
  Used to claim human control while monitoring is understaffed or alerts are unreadable. Presence without power is fiction.
practicalMeaning: |
  Define what the human can see, pause, and reverse; set alert budgets; measure intervention latency. Do not equate a dashboard with control.
