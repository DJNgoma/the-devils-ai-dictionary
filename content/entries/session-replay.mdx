  Session replay is the reconstruction of an AI or agent session from prompts, tool calls, model outputs, user actions, state changes, and logs.
whyExists: |
  Complex sessions fail in ways that are hard to understand from the final answer alone. Replay helps teams debug behaviour, audit actions, investigate incidents, and improve workflows.
misuse: |
  It gets mistaken for perfect truth. Replays depend on what was logged, redacted, retained, sampled, or lost, and they may omit exactly the detail someone later needs.
practicalMeaning: |
  In practice it means deciding which events to capture, how to protect sensitive data, how to replay without causing side effects, and how to compare what the model saw with what users saw.
