  Content credentials are metadata or attached records that describe a piece of content's origin, creation tools, edits, authorship signals, or provenance history.

  As AI-generated and edited media spread, creators, platforms, and audiences need better ways to understand how content was made and modified.

  In practice it means provenance metadata can help with disclosure and trust decisions, but only when platforms preserve it, users understand it, and policies account for its failure modes.
