  Scite is a research platform that helps users ask questions, inspect papers, and reason over scholarly literature with citation context, including whether later work supports, contrasts with, or merely mentions a claim.
whyExists: |
  It exists because scientific and scholarly research depends not only on finding papers, but on understanding how claims are situated across evidence, citations, and disagreement.
misuse: |
  It can be used as if a cited answer is automatically a settled answer. Citation-aware retrieval improves the conversation, but it does not remove the need to read methods, judge study quality, and notice when evidence is thin or contested.
practicalMeaning: |
  In practice Scite means grounded literature search, evidence summaries, claim checking, and research workflows where an AI answer is expected to point back to papers rather than float above them.
