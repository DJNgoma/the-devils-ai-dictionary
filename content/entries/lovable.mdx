  A chat-driven builder for turning "I have an idea" into a working app before anyone asks who will inherit the code.

  Lovable is a product for generating and iterating on web applications from natural-language instructions, with modes for planning, editing code, and increasingly agent-like execution across a project.
whyExists: |
  It exists because many people want software outcomes without local setup, framework literacy, or the traditional rite of discovering three build errors before lunch. Products like this collapse prototyping, editing, and deployment into one highly persuasive loop.
misuse: |
  It gets sold as though describing software and owning software are the same discipline. The tool can absolutely shorten the road to a useful prototype. It cannot repeal the maintenance burden or make architecture an optional hobby.

  In practice Lovable usually means faster internal tools, MVPs, marketing sites, and founder demos, plus a fresh opportunity to confuse visible progress with durable engineering.
