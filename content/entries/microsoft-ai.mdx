  Microsoft AI is the organisation Microsoft formed in 2024 to advance Copilot and its consumer AI products and research. It is distinct from Microsoft as a whole, and also distinct from Azure's infrastructure role or OpenAI's position as a model partner.
whyExists: |
  Because Microsoft needed a named unit for the part of its AI strategy focused on product experience, consumer interfaces, and turning model access into habitual software use.

  By using "Microsoft AI" to mean Microsoft generally, Azure specifically, or OpenAI by implication. The company has several overlapping AI layers; this term refers to the product-and-research organisation built around Copilot and related consumer experiences.
practicalMeaning: |
  In practice it usually means the organisational machinery behind Microsoft's bid to make AI a persistent interface layer across its consumer products, with Copilot as the flagship expression of that strategy.
