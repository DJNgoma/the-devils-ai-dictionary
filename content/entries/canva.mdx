plainDefinition: |
  Canva is a visual design platform for creating presentations, graphics, documents, social assets, and brand materials, with AI features that help generate, edit, resize, and adapt creative work.
whyExists: |
  It exists because most organisations need a constant stream of acceptable visual material and cannot route every banner, slide, or campaign asset through a specialist design team.
misuse: |
  It gets spoken about as if easier production equals stronger design. Templates and AI generation can improve speed and consistency, but they can also make every brand look like it was assembled from the same polite drawer.
practicalMeaning: |
  In practice Canva means self-serve creative production, brand kits, fast iteration, and AI-assisted asset generation for teams that need decent outputs quickly more often than they need bespoke art direction.
