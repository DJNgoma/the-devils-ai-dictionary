  Continued pretraining (also called domain-adaptive pretraining) further trains a base model with a language-modelling objective on additional corpora before instruction tuning or alignment.

  General models lack specialised distributional knowledge. Continued pretraining injects domain text without jumping straight to supervised tasks.

  Confused with fine-tuning on Q&A pairs. Different objective, different data needs, different failure modes like catastrophic forgetting.

  Curate domain corpora carefully, mix with general data if needed, and evaluate both domain gains and general regressions before instruction tuning.
