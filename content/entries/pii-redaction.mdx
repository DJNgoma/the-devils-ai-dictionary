  Blanking out personal data before logs and models see it, imperfectly, which is still better than shipping the passport number in cleartext.
plainDefinition: |
  PII redaction is the process of detecting and removing or masking personally identifiable information in text and artefacts before storage, training, logging, or tool calls.

  LLM pipelines copy data into prompts, traces, and fine-tunes. Redaction reduces privacy and compliance exposure.

  Treated as complete anonymisation. Detectors miss novel formats; context can re-identify; audio and images need their own handling.
practicalMeaning: |
  Combine detectors, allowlists/denylists, minimisation at collection, and access controls. Test with realistic PII forms from your domain.
