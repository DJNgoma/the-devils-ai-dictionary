  It matters because the field needed a serious reminder that capability can emerge outside the usual American shortlist, and that open releases plus aggressive price-performance claims can reset expectations very quickly.

  In practice it means more pressure on incumbents to justify their pricing, more enterprise curiosity about open-weight deployment, and more geopolitical theatre attached to what is still, at base, a model evaluation problem.
