whyExists: |
  Surveys, anecdotes, and product revenue reveal different parts of AI adoption. Large-scale usage data can show task patterns, geographic variation, workplace breadth, and whether users delegate whole jobs or seek assistance with parts of them.
misuse: |
  A large dataset is easily advertised as a census of the AI economy. ATLAS observes users and interactions within Google's products; it does not directly measure non-users, competing tools, firm output, wages, or the causal economic effect of AI. De-identification also does not remove sampling and classification choices.
