  Research tasks involve exploration, source selection, reading, comparison, and synthesis. Agents can help manage that loop when the work is too broad for a single retrieval call or chat answer.

  In practice it means source strategy, citation handling, uncertainty tracking, browsing limits, provenance, and a willingness to say that the evidence is thin.
