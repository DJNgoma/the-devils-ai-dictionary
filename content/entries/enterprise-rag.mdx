  The promise that the organisation's knowledge can be retrieved, once the organisation admits where it left it.

  Enterprise RAG is retrieval-augmented generation built over an organisation's internal documents, systems, policies, knowledge bases, and governed data sources.
whyExists: |
  General models do not know private procedures, customer records, contracts, product details, or institutional exceptions. Enterprise RAG tries to connect language models to that material without retraining them for every business question.
misuse: |
  It is sold as institutional memory when the real work is document hygiene, permissions, source freshness, connector reliability, evaluation, and deciding which department owns the answer when two official documents disagree.
practicalMeaning: |
  In practice it means source selection, ingestion pipelines, access control, chunking, ranking, citation grounding, audit trails, evaluation sets, incident handling, and a budget for finding out that the knowledge base was mostly decorative.
