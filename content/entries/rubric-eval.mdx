  Rubric eval scores model outputs using a predefined set of criteria—accuracy, completeness, tone, safety—applied by humans or automated judges.

  Vibe checks do not scale and do not explain failures. Rubrics make quality multi-dimensional and more consistent.
misuse: |
  Rubrics so vague they reintroduce vibes, or so rigid they punish valid diversity. Automated judges can misapply criteria.
practicalMeaning: |
  Write operational definitions, train judges with examples, measure inter-rater agreement, and revise rubrics when products change.
