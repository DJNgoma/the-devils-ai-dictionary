  A controlled measurement of what a system can do, as opposed to a demo where the path was paved in gold leaf.
plainDefinition: |
  A capability eval measures model or agent abilities under defined conditions—tasks, tools, time, and scoring—so claims about competence can be compared and tracked.
whyExists: |
  Marketing demos do not generalise. Capability evals try to make skill claims falsifiable.
misuse: |
  Cherry-picked tasks called 'the eval.' Capability is multi-dimensional; one number rarely captures it.
practicalMeaning: |
  Maintain a suite covering core product skills, update for contamination, and separate capability from safety evals explicitly.
