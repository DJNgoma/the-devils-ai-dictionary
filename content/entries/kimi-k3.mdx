  Kimi K3 is Moonshot AI's 2.8-trillion-parameter flagship mixture-of-experts model. It uses Kimi Delta Attention and Attention Residuals, has native visual understanding and a one-million-token context window, and targets long-horizon coding, knowledge work, reasoning, and multimodal agent tasks.

  Long software-engineering and research workflows require a model to navigate large bodies of context, use tools repeatedly, reason over visual results, and maintain progress across many steps. K3 scales model capacity and architectural sparsity around those agentic workloads.
misuse: |
  Parameter count, context length, benchmark scores, and "open" status are compressed into one claim of superiority. At launch K3 is available through Kimi products and the API, while Moonshot says the full weights will follow on 27 July 2026; hosted access is not the same thing as downloadable weights.
