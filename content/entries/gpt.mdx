  The acronym that began as a model architecture and graduated into a public synonym for whatever the chatbot did this morning.
plainDefinition: |
  GPT stands for Generative Pre-trained Transformer. In current usage it usually refers to transformer-based language models and, more specifically, to OpenAI's GPT-branded model families.
whyExists: |
  The term matters because it names a lineage of models built around pre-training and transformer architectures, but it also became a consumer-facing brand fragment attached to products, APIs, and model tiers.
misuse: |
  It gets used as a generic word for any AI assistant, any large language model, or any answer that arrived in a chat box. That makes comparisons sloppy and lets vendor branding smuggle itself into technical conversation.
practicalMeaning: |
  In practice it usually means someone is talking about a language model, often an OpenAI model, without yet being precise about product surface, model version, context window, tool access, or pricing.
