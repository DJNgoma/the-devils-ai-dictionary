  An app that begins by promising convenience and ends by wanting custody of your identity, payments, messages, bookings, workflow, and remaining dignity.

  A software product that bundles multiple services, workflows, or transaction types into a single ecosystem rather than offering one narrowly defined function.
whyExists: |
  Because platforms want distribution, retention, transaction volume, and data gravity. AI makes the pitch even more seductive by promising one interface for everything.
misuse: |
  By calling any app with four tabs a super-app. A true super-app is not just feature-bloated. It is an ecosystem strategy.
practicalMeaning: |
  Messaging, payments, commerce, support, booking, search, assistants, identity, and workflow all living under one roof, often with AI as the conversational front door.

  "The company said it was building a super-app. Translation: it wanted to become the place where users stop leaving."

AI is attractive to super-app ambitions because conversation makes sprawl feel tidy. Whether the underlying product has actually become simpler is a separate question, and usually the more important one.
