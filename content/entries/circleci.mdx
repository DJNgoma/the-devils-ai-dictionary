  CircleCI is a continuous integration and delivery platform used to build, test, and deploy software through configured pipelines.
whyExists: |
  It exists because modern software teams need repeatable checks between code and production, especially when many people, bots, and agents can all produce changes faster than humans can review them manually.
misuse: |
  It gets treated as a moral authority. A green pipeline means the configured checks passed; it does not mean the product is good, the architecture is sound, or the AI-generated change was wise.
practicalMeaning: |
  In practice CircleCI means YAML-defined workflows, test jobs, deployment gates, cache tuning, approvals, and a public record of whether a change survived the checks the team remembered to encode.
