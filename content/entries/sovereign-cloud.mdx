devilDefinition: |
  Keeping the crown jewels local, once everyone agrees where the crown, the jewels, and the admin console are.
plainDefinition: |
  Sovereign cloud is a cloud deployment or operating model designed to satisfy jurisdiction, data residency, operational control, access, security, and regulatory requirements for a country, region, sector, or institution.
whyExists: |
  It exists because governments and regulated organisations want cloud and AI capability without surrendering every sensitive dependency to foreign law, remote operators, opaque support paths, or control planes they cannot meaningfully govern.
misuse: |
  It is misused when a local region, local reseller, or patriotic brand treatment is presented as sovereignty. Data residency helps, but sovereignty also depends on who operates the service, who can access keys and telemetry, which law applies, and who can turn the lights off.
practicalMeaning: |
  In practice it means reviewing legal jurisdiction, local operations, key custody, support access, metadata flows, disaster recovery, vendor dependency, hardware supply, audit rights, and whether AI services inherit the same controls as ordinary storage and compute.
