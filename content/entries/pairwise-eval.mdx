  Pairwise eval compares two model outputs head-to-head under the same prompt, producing a preference rather than an absolute grade, commonly used with human or model judges.

  Absolute scoring is hard and noisy. Humans are often better at choosing the better of two than assigning consistent points.

  Treated as bias-free. Position bias, verbosity bias, and judge model bias still skew results.

  Randomise order, control for length, use multiple judges, and report win rates with confidence intervals—not just a winner trophy.
