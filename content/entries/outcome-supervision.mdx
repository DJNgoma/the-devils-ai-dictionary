devilDefinition: |
  Only marking the final answer right or wrong, which is cheap, common, and how lucky guesses get tenure.
plainDefinition: |
  Outcome supervision provides labels or rewards solely on final results—correct answer, successful trajectory end state—without grading intermediate steps.
whyExists: |
  Final labels are easier to collect at scale than full process traces. Much of modern post-training still depends on them.
misuse: |
  Assumed sufficient for teaching robust reasoning. Models can hack outcomes with shallow strategies.
practicalMeaning: |
  Use outcome supervision where checkers exist; add process supervision or verifiers for multi-step domains where shortcuts abound.
