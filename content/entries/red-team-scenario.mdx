  A red-team scenario is a specific adversarial or misuse situation designed to test how an AI system behaves under pressure, attack, ambiguity, or abuse.
whyExists: |
  Red-teaming needs more than general concern. Scenarios make risks testable by specifying the actor, goal, context, constraints, and expected safety behaviour.
misuse: |
  It gets turned into theatre when scenarios are vague, unrealistic, unmeasured, or selected because they sound dramatic in a workshop.
practicalMeaning: |
  In practice it means writing testable cases that reflect real misuse paths, then using the results to improve controls, policies, monitoring, and incident response.

  "The red-team scenario tested whether a malicious document could persuade the agent to leak CRM data, which was more useful than saying 'what about bad prompts' for an hour."
