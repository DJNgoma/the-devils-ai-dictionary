  A Forward Deployed Engineer is an embedded technical role that works directly with customers or field teams to implement, customise, integrate, and adapt software in real operational environments.
whyExists: |
  Complex AI and enterprise systems often fail in the gap between product promises and customer reality. FDEs sit near that gap, translating messy workflows into working implementations and product feedback.

  The role gets romanticised as heroic execution while sometimes masking a product that needs too much bespoke labour to work repeatably.

  In practice it means customer proximity, integration work, rapid prototyping, domain learning, support escalation, and a constant tension between scalable product and customised delivery.
